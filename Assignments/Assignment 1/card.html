SUPER STAR

RAJNIKANTH

Often called Superstar, Rajinikanth is one of India’s most iconic actors, known for his unique style, charismatic screen presence, and mass appeal. His films often blend action, emotion, and larger-than-life heroism.

ULAGANAYAGAN

KAMAL HASSAN

Kamal Haasan is considered one of Indian cinema’s greatest actors, known for his unmatched versatility, dedication, and innovation. With a career spanning decades, he has delivered classics across genres, excelling in acting, writing, and filmmaking. His commitment to storytelling and artistic excellence sets him apart as a true cinema legend.

THALA

AJITH KUMAR

Ajith is celebrated for his understated charm, sincere performances, and strong fanbase. Known for balancing action and emotional depth, he is also respected for his off-screen humility and passion for racing.

THALAPHATHY

VIJAY

Vijay is one of Tamil cinema’s biggest stars, known for his energetic screen presence, dance skills, and mass-appeal action films. His movies often focus on social themes and his performances connect strongly with youth audiences.

NADIPU NAYAGAN

SURYA

Suriya is admired for his versatility and dedication, excelling in both commercial films and critically acclaimed roles. Known for movies like Ghajini, Soorarai Pottru, and Jai Bhim, he combines strong performances with social awareness.

ATMAN

SILAMBARASAN

Simbu is known for his natural acting, musical talent, and youthful screen presence. He has delivered impactful performances and is appreciated for his versatility in romance, drama, and action films.

YOUNG SUPER STAR

DHANUSH

Dhanush is praised for his natural acting, unique screen presence, and versatility across genres. From intense dramas to lighthearted roles, he brings authenticity to every character. His work in films like Aadukalam, Asuran, and his successful ventures in music and direction highlight his multifaceted talent.

PRINCE

SIVAKARTHIKEYAN

Sivakarthikeyan is loved for his charming personality, humor, and relatable performances. Rising from television to become a major film star, he excels in family entertainers and comedy-dramas. His natural comic timing, heartfelt roles, and growing versatility have earned him a loyal fan base.
